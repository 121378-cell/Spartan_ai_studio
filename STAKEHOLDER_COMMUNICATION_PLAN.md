## 🎯 KEY STAKEHOLDERS & ROLES

### Executive Leadership:
- **CEO:** Strategic direction, budget approval, major milestone sign-off

- **Investors:** Progress updates, milestone achievements
- **Technology Partners:** Integration support, API access

## 📅 COMMUNICATION SCHEDULE

### Weekly Executive Updates:
**Audience:** CEO, CTO, CFO, Product VP  
**Frequency:** Every Friday 3:00 PM  
**Format:** 30-minute presentation + Q&A  
**Content:**
- Progress against milestones
- Budget spend vs. allocation
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Resource needs and constraints
- Next week priorities

### Bi-weekly Technical Reviews:
**Audience:** Engineering teams, CTO, Product Manager  
**Frequency:** Every other Tuesday 10:00 AM  
**Format:** 60-minute deep dive session  
**Content:**
- Code quality metrics
- Performance benchmarks
- Security assessments
- Technical debt status
- Architecture decisions

### Monthly Stakeholder Meetings:
**Audience:** All internal stakeholders + key external partners  
**Frequency:** First Monday of each month 2:00 PM  
**Format:** 90-minute comprehensive review  
**Content:**
- Project health dashboard
- Market feedback integration
- Competitive landscape analysis
- Resource planning for next month
- Strategic direction adjustments

## 🚨 APPROVAL PROCESS WORKFLOW

### Tier 1 Approvals (Immediate - Within 24 hours):
**Required for:**
- Emergency budget allocations (<$5K)
- Critical bug fixes affecting production
- Urgent security patches
- Team member overtime approvals

**Process:**
1. Submit request with justification to direct supervisor
2. Supervisor evaluates and forwards to appropriate approver
3. Decision communicated within 24 hours
4. Documentation filed in project management system

### Tier 2 Approvals (Standard - 3-5 business days):
**Required for:**
- Feature scope changes
- Budget allocations ($5K-$50K)
- New hire approvals
- Third-party vendor contracts
- Major technical architecture decisions

**Process:**
1. Formal proposal with business case submitted
2. Review by relevant department heads
3. Presentation to steering committee
4. Written approval documentation
5. Implementation timeline established

### Tier 3 Approvals (Strategic - 1-2 weeks):
**Required for:**
- Major product direction changes
- Budget allocations >$50K
- Partnership agreements
- Market expansion initiatives
- Fundamental technology shifts

**Process:**
1. Comprehensive business case development
2. Cross-functional impact assessment
3. Board/president review meeting
4. Formal approval vote/documentation
5. Communication to all stakeholders

## 📞 COMMUNICATION CHANNELS

### Primary Channels:
- **Slack:** Real-time team communication (#spartan-hub-project)
- **Email:** Formal communications and documentation
- **Jira:** Task tracking and progress updates
- **Confluence:** Documentation and knowledge sharing
- **Zoom:** Video meetings and presentations

### Escalation Pathways:
1. **Team Level:** Direct supervisor
2. **Department Level:** Department head
3. **Executive Level:** CTO/Project Director
4. **Board Level:** CEO/President (for strategic issues)

### Crisis Communication:
- **Immediate Issues:** Slack emergency channel + phone escalation
- **Serious Problems:** 1-hour response time to executive team
- **Critical Situations:** 15-minute response to CEO/CTO
