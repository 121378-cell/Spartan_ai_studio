# Video Tutorial Plans

## Overview
This document outlines the planned video tutorials for the Spartan Hub platform. These tutorials will help users understand and utilize all features effectively.

## Tutorial Series Structure

### Beginner Series (5 videos)
Designed for new users who are just starting with the platform.

1. **Getting Started with Spartan Hub** (3-5 minutes)
   - Platform overview and benefits
   - Account creation and setup
   - Navigating the dashboard
   - Setting up your profile and goals

2. **Connecting Your Health Devices** (4-6 minutes)
   - Overview of supported devices (Garmin, Google Fit)
   - Step-by-step connection process
   - Troubleshooting common connection issues
   - Understanding synced data

3. **Your First Workout** (5-7 minutes)
   - Browsing and selecting workouts
   - Understanding workout descriptions
   - Starting and tracking a workout
   - Reviewing completed workout data

4. **Understanding Your Dashboard** (4-6 minutes)
   - Dashboard elements and widgets
   - Reading daily metrics
   - Setting up quick actions
   - Customizing your view

5. **Managing Your Account** (3-5 minutes)
   - Updating profile information
   - Changing password and security settings
   - Managing notifications
   - Subscription and billing basics

### Intermediate Series (4 videos)
For users familiar with basics who want to maximize platform features.

1. **Introduction to Video Form Analysis** (6-8 minutes)
   - How the technology works
   - Benefits of form analysis
   - Supported exercises (squat, deadlift)
   - Setting up your environment

2. **Perfecting Your Squat Form** (8-10 minutes)
   - Proper setup for squat analysis
   - Understanding squat metrics
   - Common mistakes and corrections
   - Interpreting feedback and scores

3. **Mastering Your Deadlift Technique** (8-10 minutes)
   - Optimal positioning for deadlift analysis
   - Key metrics to focus on
   - Correcting common deadlift errors
   - Progressive improvement strategies

4. **Reviewing Your Performance Data** (6-8 minutes)
   - Navigating analytics dashboards
   - Understanding trends and insights
   - Setting performance goals
   - Exporting and sharing data

### Advanced Series (3 videos)
For power users looking to leverage all platform capabilities.

1. **Advanced Analytics and Insights** (8-10 minutes)
   - Deep dive into performance metrics
   - Understanding correlation data
   - Using insights for training optimization
   - Identifying patterns and trends

2. **Customizing Workouts and Programs** (7-9 minutes)
   - Creating custom workouts
   - Building personalized training programs
   - Sharing and importing workouts
   - Advanced workout settings

3. **Maximizing Health Data Integration** (6-8 minutes)
   - Advanced health metric interpretation
   - Correlating health data with performance
   - Setting up automated insights
   - Privacy and data management best practices

## Technical Specifications

### Video Format
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p) minimum
- Frame Rate: 30 fps
- Codec: H.264
- Audio: AAC, 128 kbps minimum
- Format: MP4 for web delivery

### Accessibility
- Closed captions in English (and other languages as needed)
- Audio descriptions for visual elements
- Transcripts available for each video
- High contrast text overlays where applicable

### Equipment Needed
- Screen recording software (OBS Studio, Camtasia, or similar)
- High-quality microphone for voiceovers
- Video editing software
- Graphics software for annotations and callouts
- Green screen setup (optional, for advanced videos)

## Content Guidelines

### Script Standards
- Conversational tone, avoiding jargon
- Clear, step-by-step instructions
- Visual cues synchronized with narration
- Estimated timing for each segment
- Placeholder for b-roll footage

### Visual Standards
- Consistent branding and colors
- Clean, readable text overlays
- Highlight important UI elements
- Smooth transitions between screens
- Professional quality throughout

### Voiceover Guidelines
- Clear, professional narration
- Appropriate pacing (not too fast or slow)
- Consistent volume levels
- Minimal background noise
- Enthusiastic but not overly promotional tone
