# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Spartan Hub?
Spartan Hub is an AI-powered fitness coaching platform that combines workout tracking, health data integration, and advanced video form analysis to help you achieve your fitness goals safely and effectively.

### How does the Video Form Analysis work?
Our Video Form Analysis uses computer vision and machine learning to analyze your exercise form in real-time. The system tracks your joints and movement patterns to provide instant feedback on your technique.

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All personal data is encrypted and stored securely. We do not sell your data to third parties. Video feeds are processed locally when possible and are only stored with your explicit permission.

### What devices are compatible?
Spartan Hub works on modern web browsers (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+) on desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones with cameras.

## Video Form Analysis

### Which exercises are supported?
Currently, we support Squat and Deadlift analysis. More exercises including Bench Press, Overhead Press, and Row will be added in future updates.

### Why isn't my camera working?
First, ensure you've granted camera permissions when prompted by your browser. Check that no other applications are using your camera. If problems persist, try refreshing the page or restarting your browser.

### How accurate is the form analysis?
Our system has been trained on thousands of exercise repetitions and achieves high accuracy under optimal conditions. Accuracy may vary based on lighting, clothing, and camera positioning. We recommend using good lighting and wearing form-fitting clothes for best results.

### What do the form scores mean?
Form scores range from 0 to 100, with higher scores indicating better form. The score is calculated based on multiple factors including depth, alignment, and movement quality. Focus on gradual improvement rather than achieving perfect scores immediately.

### Can I save and review my analysis sessions?
Yes, all your analysis sessions are saved to your account. You can review your historical form scores, track your progress over time, and compare your technique across sessions.

### How can I improve my form score?
Focus on the specific metrics highlighted in your feedback. Practice movements slowly at first, pay attention to the coaching recommendations, and gradually increase your speed as your form improves. Consistency is more important than perfection.

## Health Data Integration

### Which devices and platforms do you support?
We currently support Garmin Connect and Google Fit. We're continuously working to add support for more devices including Apple Health, Oura Ring, and other popular fitness trackers.

### How often does health data sync?
Health data typically syncs every 2 hours automatically. You can also trigger a manual sync from the Health Data section of your account.

### What health metrics do you track?
We track steps, heart rate, sleep patterns, calories burned, and other metrics depending on your connected devices. All data is aggregated to provide insights into your recovery and readiness for workouts.

### My health data isn't syncing correctly. What should I do?
First, verify that your device is properly connected and online. Try disconnecting and reconnecting the device. If problems persist, contact support with details about your device and the specific metrics that aren't syncing.

## Workouts & Training

### How do I find workouts?
Browse workouts in the "Workouts" section where you can filter by type, difficulty, equipment, and duration. You can also use our AI coach to recommend workouts based on your goals and fitness level.

### Can I create my own workouts?
Yes, premium subscribers can create custom workouts using the "Create Workout" feature. You can add exercises, set durations and intensities, and save your routines for future use.

### How do I log a workout manually?
Go to "Workouts" > "Log Workout" to manually enter details of a workout you completed outside the app. You can record duration, distance, calories, and other relevant metrics.

### What if I miss a scheduled workout?
Don't worry! You can adjust your schedule from the calendar view, or simply resume where you left off. Our adaptive system will adjust recommendations based on your actual activity.

## Troubleshooting

### The app is running slowly
Close other browser tabs and applications, check your internet connection, and clear your browser cache. If using a mobile device, ensure you have sufficient storage space available.

### I can't access certain features
Some features require a premium subscription. Check your subscription status in "Account Settings". If you believe you should have access to a feature, contact support for assistance.

### The app keeps crashing or freezing
Try refreshing the page first. If problems persist, clear your browser cache and cookies, restart your browser, or try using a different browser. Make sure you're using a supported browser version.

### I'm not receiving email notifications
Check your spam folder and ensure our emails aren't being filtered. In "Account Settings" > "Notifications", verify that email notifications are enabled and your email address is correct.

### How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team by:
- Using the in-app chat feature (available 9AM-6PM EST)
- Emailing support@spartanhub.com
- Submitting a ticket through our support portal

## Billing & Payments

### When will I be charged?
For monthly subscriptions, you'll be charged on the same day each month as your initial signup. Annual subscribers are charged once per year on the anniversary of their signup date.

### Can I get a refund?
We offer refunds within 30 days of purchase for annual subscriptions. Monthly subscriptions can be canceled at any time, and your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and PayPal. Payment information is processed securely through our payment provider.

### Will my price change after my subscription?
Your subscription price remains the same for the duration of your subscription. Price changes only apply to new customers or when you choose to upgrade or downgrade your plan.

## Privacy & Security

### What data do you collect?
We collect data necessary to provide our services, including workout logs, health metrics from connected devices, and form analysis data. We do not collect personal conversations or unnecessary personal information.

### How can I delete my account?
Go to "Account Settings" > "Privacy" > "Delete Account". Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

### Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export your personal data by going to "Account Settings" > "Privacy" > "Export Data". You'll receive a download link via email with your data in a standard format.

### Do you share my data with third parties?
We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research purposes, but never individual user data without your explicit consent.
