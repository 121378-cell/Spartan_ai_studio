## Getting Started

### System Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+)
- Stable internet connection
- Device with camera for video form analysis
- Microphone for voice commands (optional)

### Installation & Access
1. Visit [your-spartan-hub-url.com] in your preferred browser
2. Click "Sign Up" to create a new account or "Log In" if you already have one
3. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your registration
4. Verify your email address using the link sent to your inbox

## Account Setup

1. Navigate to "Profile" in the main menu

1. Go to "Goals" section
2. Set your primary goal (long-term)

4. Set timeline for achievement

## Video Form Analysis

### Overview
The Video Form Analysis feature uses computer vision and AI to analyze your exercise form in real-time. This helps prevent injuries and improves exercise effectiveness.

### Supported Exercises
- **Squat**: Analyzes depth, knee tracking, back angle, and balance
- **Deadlift**: Evaluates bar path, hip hinge, back angle, and lockout position

### How to Use Video Form Analysis

#### Starting a Session
1. Navigate to "Video Analysis" in the main menu
2. Select the exercise you want to analyze (Squat or Deadlift)
3. Grant camera permissions when prompted
4. Position yourself in the frame as indicated by the overlay
5. Click "Start Recording" to begin the analysis

#### During the Session
- Maintain good lighting conditions
- Wear form-fitting clothing for better detection
- Perform exercises in the center of the frame
- Watch the live feedback on your form quality
- Aim for a higher form score by correcting your technique

#### Understanding the Results
- **Form Score (0-100)**: Overall assessment of your exercise form
- **Detailed Metrics**: Specific measurements for each aspect of the movement
- **Warnings**: Identified issues with your form
- **Coaching Recommendations**: Suggestions for improvement
- **Historical Trends**: Comparison with your previous sessions

### Tips for Best Results
- Use in well-lit environments
- Position camera at eye level
- Ensure full body visibility in frame
- Perform movements at moderate speed
- Repeat exercises multiple times for consistent data

## Health Data Integration

### Connecting Wearables
Spartan Hub supports integration with various health devices and platforms:

#### Garmin Connect
1. Go to "Health Data" > "Connect Devices"
2. Select "Garmin Connect"
3. Enter your Garmin credentials
4. Authorize data sharing
5. Select data types to sync (steps, heart rate, sleep, etc.)

#### Google Fit
1. Navigate to "Health Data" > "Connect Devices"
2. Choose "Google Fit"
3. Sign in to your Google account
4. Grant necessary permissions
5. Configure sync frequency

### Viewing Health Data
- Access "Health Data" tab to see integrated information
- View historical trends and correlations
- Compare health metrics with workout performance
- Adjust training intensity based on recovery indicators

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Camera Not Working
- Check browser permissions for camera access
- Ensure no other applications are using the camera
- Refresh the page and try again
- Update your browser to the latest version

#### Poor Form Detection
- Ensure adequate lighting
- Wear form-fitting clothes
- Position yourself properly in the frame
- Check for obstructions in the background

#### Sync Issues with Wearables
- Verify device connectivity
- Check internet connection
- Re-authorize device connections
- Contact support if issues persist

#### Slow Performance
- Close other browser tabs and applications
- Check your internet connection speed
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Restart your browser

### Contact Support
If you continue experiencing issues:
- Email: support@spartanhub.com
- In-app chat support (available 9AM-6PM EST)
- Submit a ticket through the support portal
