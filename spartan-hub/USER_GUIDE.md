## 🎯 Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit https://spartanhub.io
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Verify your email
5. Complete your profile

### First Workout

1. Click "Start Workout"
2. Choose your exercise type
3. Set up your camera
4. Perform your exercise
5. View your form analysis

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

### Form Tips

1. **Warm up properly** before heavy lifts
2. **Start with light weight** to master form
3. **Record regularly** to track improvements
4. **Follow recommendations** to prevent injuries

### Workout Tips

1. **Consistency is key** - aim for regular workouts
2. **Progressive overload** - gradually increase weight
3. **Rest and recovery** - allow muscle recovery
4. **Proper nutrition** - fuel your workouts

### Challenge Tips

1. **Choose appropriate** difficulty level
2. **Track progress** daily
3. **Stay consistent** throughout challenge
4. **Celebrate milestones** along the way

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: How accurate is form analysis?

A: Our AI-powered form analysis is 90%+ accurate for supported exercises.

### Q: Can I use Spartan Hub without a camera?

A: Yes, but you'll miss out on form analysis features.

### Q: How do I join a team?

A: Browse teams and click "Request to Join" or accept an invitation.

### Q: What happens if I miss a day?

A: Your streak resets, but you can start a new one immediately.
