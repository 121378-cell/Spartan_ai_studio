## 🚀 GETTING STARTED

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit **https://spartan-hub.com** (or your instance URL)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right corner
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Verify your email (check inbox for verification link)

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

1. Log in with your credentials
2. Navigate to **Profile** → **Settings**
3. Complete your fitness profile:
   - Age
   - Gender
   - Height
   - Weight
   - Fitness Level (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced)
   - Fitness Goals

### Step 3: Connect Wearable Devices (Optional)

1. Go to **Settings** → **Wearables**
2. Choose your device:
   - **Garmin** - Connect Garmin account
   - **Apple Health** - iOS health data
   - **Google Fit** - Android health data
3. Follow the OAuth authorization flow
4. Grant necessary permissions

### Step 4: Start Your First Workout

1. Navigate to **Workouts** → **Start Workout**
2. Choose a workout type
3. Enable camera for form analysis (optional)
4. Begin your workout!

## 🎥 VIDEO FORM ANALYSIS

### What is Video Form Analysis?

Video Form Analysis uses **AI-powered computer vision** to analyze your exercise form in real-time and provide instant feedback.

### Supported Exercises

| Exercise | Analysis Points | Difficulty |
|----------|----------------|------------|
| **Squat** | Knee angle, hip depth, back angle | Beginner |
| **Deadlift** | Back curvature, bar path, hip height | Intermediate |
| **Lunge** | Knee alignment, stride length | Beginner |
| **Push-Up** | Body alignment, elbow angle | Beginner |
| **Row** | Back position, elbow path | Intermediate |
| **Plank** | Body straight line, hip sag | Beginner |

### How to Use Video Analysis

**Step 1: Start Analysis**
1. Go to **Workouts** → **Video Analysis**
2. Select the exercise you want to analyze
3. Position your device/camera
4. Click **"Start Recording"**

**Step 2: Perform Exercise**
1. Perform 3-5 repetitions with good form
2. Ensure full body is visible in frame
3. Maintain consistent lighting
4. Keep camera stationary

**Step 3: Review Results**
1. Wait for AI analysis (5-10 seconds)
2. Review form score (0-100%)
3. Read detailed feedback
4. Watch the pose overlay visualization

**Step 4: Apply Feedback**
1. Read specific recommendations
2. Watch the ghost frame comparison
3. Try again with adjustments
4. Track improvement over time

### Understanding Your Form Score

| Score | Rating | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **90-100%** | Excellent | Perfect form, minimal risk |
| **80-89%** | Good | Minor adjustments needed |
| **70-79%** | Fair | Several improvements needed |
| **60-69%** | Poor | Significant form issues |
| **<60%** | Needs Work | High injury risk, stop and correct |

### Tips for Best Results

✅ **DO:**
- Use good lighting (natural or bright indoor)
- Wear form-fitting clothing
- Keep camera at proper distance (6-10 feet)
- Ensure full body is in frame
- Perform exercises at normal speed

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use in dark or poorly lit areas
- Wear baggy clothing (hides body position)
- Move the camera during recording
- Cut off body parts in frame
- Rush through repetitions

## ⌚ WEARABLE INTEGRATION

### Supported Devices

Spartan Hub integrates with:

| Platform | Devices | Data Synced |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **Garmin** | Garmin watches, trackers | Steps, HR, sleep, workouts |
| **Apple Health** | iPhone, Apple Watch | All health data |
| **Google Fit** | Android phones, Wear OS | Steps, HR, activities |

### Connecting Your Device

**Garmin:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Wearables**
2. Click **"Connect Garmin"**
3. Log in to Garmin account
4. Grant permissions
5. Click **"Authorize"**

**Apple Health (iOS):**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Wearables**
2. Click **"Connect Apple Health"**
3. Review permissions
4. Click **"Allow"**
5. Select data categories to share

**Google Fit (Android):**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Wearables**
2. Click **"Connect Google Fit"**
3. Choose Google account
4. Grant permissions
5. Click **"Allow"**

### Synced Data

| Data Type | Garmin | Apple Health | Google Fit |
|-----------|--------|--------------|------------|
| Steps | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Heart Rate | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Sleep | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Workouts | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Calories | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Distance | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Weight | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |

### Sync Frequency

- **Automatic:** Every 15 minutes when app is open
- **Background:** Every hour (if permissions granted)
- **Manual:** Click **"Sync Now"** in Settings

### Troubleshooting Sync Issues

**Problem:** Device not syncing

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify device is connected (Settings → Wearables)
3. Click **"Sync Now"** manually
4. Disconnect and reconnect device
5. Check device battery level

**Problem:** Missing data

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure all permissions are granted
2. Check device is recording data
3. Wait up to 1 hour for sync
4. Contact support if issue persists

## 💪 WORKOUT TRACKING

### Starting a Workout

1. Navigate to **Workouts** → **Start Workout**
2. Choose workout type:
   - **Strength Training**
   - **Cardio**
   - **HIIT**
   - **Yoga/Stretching**
   - **Custom Workout**
3. Add exercises (if strength training)
4. Click **"Start Workout"**

### During Workout

**Log Exercises:**
- Exercise name
- Sets, reps, weight
- Rest time between sets
- Notes (optional)

**Enable Form Analysis:**
- Toggle **"Analyze Form"** for any exercise

**Track Metrics:**
- Heart rate (from wearable)
- Calories burned
- Workout duration
- Rest time

### Completing Workout

1. Click **"Finish Workout"**
2. Review workout summary:
   - Total volume
   - Duration
   - Calories burned
   - Average heart rate

4. Rate workout difficulty
5. Click **"Save Workout"**

### Workout History

View all past workouts:
1. Go to **Workouts** → **History**
2. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - Workout type
   - Specific exercises
3. Click any workout to view details

## 📈 PROGRESS & ANALYTICS

### Progress Dashboard

Access at **Progress** → **Dashboard**

**Metrics Tracked:**
- Strength gains (1RM estimates)
- Workout frequency
- Volume progression
- Body composition (if logged)
- Cardiovascular improvements

### Analytics Features

**1. Performance Trends**
- Weekly/monthly charts
- Exercise-specific progress
- Personal records (PRs)

**2. ML Forecasts**
- Injury risk assessment
- Performance predictions
- Recovery recommendations

**3. Readiness Score**
- Daily readiness (0-100%)
- Based on sleep, HRV, recent training
- Training recommendations

### Understanding ML Insights

**Injury Risk:**
- **Low (0-30%):** Train normally
- **Moderate (31-60%):** Monitor form, reduce volume
- **High (61-80%):** Active recovery recommended
- **Very High (81-100%):** Rest required

**Performance Forecast:**
- Shows predicted performance in 4, 8, 12 weeks
- Based on current training patterns
- Helps set realistic goals

**Recovery Time:**
- Estimated recovery after workouts
- Considers workout intensity, sleep, stress
- Guides next workout timing

## 🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING

### Common Issues

**Issue: Can't log in**

**Solutions:**
1. Verify email and password
2. Check caps lock
3. Click "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try incognito/private mode
6. Contact support if issue persists

**Issue: Video analysis not working**

**Solutions:**
1. Check camera permissions
2. Ensure good lighting
3. Verify full body is in frame
4. Try a different browser
5. Check internet connection
6. Update to latest browser version

**Issue: Wearable not syncing**

**Solutions:**
1. Check device is connected (Settings → Wearables)
2. Verify permissions granted
3. Click "Sync Now" manually
4. Disconnect and reconnect device
5. Check device has data
6. Wait up to 1 hour for sync

**Issue: AI Coach not responding**

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different question format
5. Wait a few moments (AI may be busy)

**Issue: Slow performance**

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet speed
2. Close other browser tabs
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Check if issue persists on mobile
