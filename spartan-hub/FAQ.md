## ACCOUNT & REGISTRATION

### Q: Is Spartan Hub free to use?

**A:** Yes! Spartan Hub offers:
- ✅ **Free Tier:** Full access to core features
- ✅ **Premium Tier:** Advanced features (coming soon)

**Free Tier Includes:**
- Unlimited workouts
- Video form analysis (5 per day)
- AI Coach Vitalis (basic)
- Wearable integration
- Progress tracking

### Q: How do I create an account?

**A:** 
1. Visit the signup page
2. Enter name, email, password
3. Verify your email
4. Complete fitness profile
5. Start training!

### Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

**A:**
1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password
5. Log in with new password

### Q: Can I have multiple accounts?

**A:** We recommend one account per person. Multiple accounts can cause:
- Data fragmentation
- Sync issues with wearables
- Confusion in progress tracking

### Q: How do I delete my account?

**A:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Scroll to **"Delete Account"**
3. Click **"Delete My Account"**
4. Confirm deletion
5. All data will be deleted within 30 days

## VIDEO FORM ANALYSIS

### Q: How does video form analysis work?

**A:** Our AI uses computer vision (MediaPipe) to:
1. Detect body landmarks (33 points)
2. Calculate joint angles
3. Compare to ideal form
4. Provide instant feedback
5. Track improvement over time

### Q: What exercises can I analyze?

**A:** Currently supported:
- ✅ Squat
- ✅ Deadlift
- ✅ Lunge
- ✅ Push-Up
- ✅ Row
- ✅ Plank

More exercises coming soon!

### Q: Do I need special equipment?

**A:** No! You only need:
- A smartphone, tablet, or webcam
- Good lighting
- Enough space to move
- Internet connection

### Q: How accurate is the analysis?

**A:** 
- **Accuracy:** 90-95% for standard exercises
- **Frame Rate:** 30 fps on most devices
- **Processing Time:** 5-10 seconds per analysis

**Factors affecting accuracy:**
- Lighting quality
- Camera angle
- Clothing (form-fitting is better)
- Video quality

### Q: Can I use video analysis without internet?

**A:** No, video analysis requires internet connection because:
- AI processing happens on our servers
- Results are saved to your profile
- Comparison data is stored in the cloud

### Q: How many video analyses can I do per day?

**A:**
- **Free Tier:** 5 analyses per day
- **Premium Tier:** Unlimited (coming soon)

### Q: What if the analysis is wrong?

**A:** AI is not perfect. If you disagree:
1. Review the feedback critically
2. Try re-recording with better lighting
3. Consult a real-life trainer
4. Report issue to support

**Remember:** AI feedback is guidance, not medical advice.

## AI COACH VITALIS

### Q: What is Coach Vitalis?

**A:** Coach Vitalis is your AI-powered fitness coach that provides:
- Personalized workout recommendations
- Form and technique advice
- Nutrition guidance
- Recovery recommendations
- Motivation and support

### Q: Is Coach Vitalis a real person?

**A:** No, Coach Vitalis is an AI assistant powered by advanced language models. However:
- Responses are based on exercise science
- Information is evidence-based
- Recommendations are personalized to you

### Q: Can I trust the advice?

**A:** Coach Vitalis provides:
- ✅ Evidence-based information
- ✅ General fitness guidance
- ✅ Educational content

**However:**
- ❌ Not a replacement for medical advice
- ❌ Not a certified personal trainer
- ❌ May not know your specific limitations

**Always consult:**
- Doctor for medical concerns
- Physical therapist for injuries
- Certified trainer for personalized programs

### Q: What can I ask Coach Vitalis?

**A:** Almost anything fitness-related:
- "How do I squat properly?"
- "What's a good pre-workout meal?"
- "Create a 4-week strength program"
- "Why am I always sore?"
- "How do I stay motivated?"

### Q: Is Coach Vitalis available 24/7?

**A:** Yes! Coach Vitalis is available:
- 24 hours a day
- 7 days a week
- 365 days a year

**Response Time:** Usually instant, may take 5-10 seconds during peak times.

### Q: Can Coach Vitalis see my workout history?

**A:** Yes, Coach Vitalis has access to:
- Your workout history
- Performance data
- Goals and preferences
- Biometric data (if connected)

This allows for personalized recommendations.

## WEARABLE INTEGRATION

### Q: Which devices are supported?

**A:**
- ✅ **Garmin:** All Garmin fitness devices
- ✅ **Apple Health:** iPhone and Apple Watch
- ✅ **Google Fit:** Android phones and Wear OS

### Q: How often does data sync?

**A:**
- **Automatic:** Every 15 minutes (app open)
- **Background:** Every hour (if permitted)
- **Manual:** Click "Sync Now" anytime

### Q: Is my wearable data private?

**A:** Yes! Your data is:
- Encrypted in transit and at rest
- Never sold to third parties
- Only used to improve your experience
- Accessible only to you

### Q: What if my device isn't syncing?

**A:** Try these steps:
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify device is connected (Settings → Wearables)
3. Click "Sync Now" manually
4. Disconnect and reconnect device
5. Check device has data recorded
6. Wait up to 1 hour
7. Contact support if issue persists

### Q: Can I export my data?

**A:** Yes!
1. Go to **Settings** → **Data**
2. Click **"Export Data"**
3. Choose format (JSON, CSV)
4. Download your data

## PROGRESS & ANALYTICS

### Q: How is my progress calculated?

**A:** Progress is based on:
- Workout performance
- Strength gains
- Consistency
- Wearable data
- Form improvements

### Q: What is the readiness score?

**A:** Readiness score (0-100%) indicates how prepared you are for training:
- **80-100%:** Ready for intense training
- **60-79%:** Normal training OK
- **40-59%:** Light training recommended
- **<40%:** Rest or active recovery

Based on sleep, HRV, recent training, and stress.

### Q: What are ML forecasts?

**A:** Machine Learning forecasts predict:
- Future performance (4, 8, 12 weeks)
- Injury risk probability
- Optimal recovery time
- Training load recommendations

### Q: How accurate are ML predictions?

**A:** 
- **Short-term (1-4 weeks):** 80-85% accuracy
- **Medium-term (1-3 months):** 70-75% accuracy
- **Long-term (3+ months):** 60-65% accuracy

**Remember:** These are predictions, not guarantees.

### Q: Can I track body measurements?

**A:** Yes! Track:
- Weight
- Body fat percentage
- Muscle mass
- Measurements (arms, waist, etc.)

Go to **Progress** → **Body Metrics** to log.

## TECHNICAL ISSUES

### Q: Why is the app slow?

**A:** Common causes:
- Slow internet connection
- Browser cache needs clearing
- Too many browser tabs open
- Outdated browser

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet speed
2. Clear browser cache
3. Close other tabs
4. Update browser
5. Try different browser

### Q: Video analysis won't start. What do I do?

**A:**
1. Check camera permissions
2. Ensure good lighting
3. Try a different browser
4. Clear browser cache
5. Check internet connection
6. Restart your device

### Q: I'm getting error messages. What do I do?

**A:**
1. Note the exact error message
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try incognito/private mode
5. Contact support with error details

### Q: Can I use Spartan Hub offline?

**A:** Limited offline functionality:
- ✅ Log workouts (syncs when online)
- ✅ View past workouts
- ❌ Video analysis (requires internet)
- ❌ AI Coach (requires internet)
- ❌ Wearable sync (requires internet)

### Q: What browsers are supported?

**A:**
- ✅ Chrome (latest)
- ✅ Firefox (latest)
- ✅ Safari (latest)
- ✅ Edge (latest)

**Not supported:**
- ❌ Internet Explorer
- ❌ Old browser versions
