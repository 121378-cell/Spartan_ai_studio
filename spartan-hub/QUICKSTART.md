**Version:** 2.0.0

---

## ⏱️ QUICK START OVERVIEW

Get up and running with Spartan Hub in just 5 minutes:

```
Minute 1:  Create Account
Minute 2:  Complete Profile
Minute 3:  Set Goals
Minute 4:  Explore Dashboard
Minute 5:  Start First Workout
```

---

## MINUTE 1: CREATE ACCOUNT

### Step 1.1: Sign Up

1. Visit **https://spartan-hub.com** (or your instance)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** button (top right)
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** Your full name
   - **Email:** Valid email address
   - **Password:** Minimum 8 characters

### Step 1.2: Verify Email

1. Check your email inbox
2. Look for email from "Spartan Hub"
3. Click **"Verify Email"** button
4. You'll be redirected to login page

**⏰ Time Elapsed:** ~1 minute

---

## MINUTE 2: COMPLETE PROFILE

### Step 2.1: Log In

1. Enter your email and password
2. Click **"Log In"**

### Step 2.2: Fitness Profile

1. Click **"Complete Profile"** when prompted
2. Enter:
   - **Age:** Your age
   - **Gender:** Male/Female/Other
   - **Height:** cm or ft/in
   - **Weight:** kg or lbs
   - **Fitness Level:** Choose one:
     - 🟢 Beginner (0-6 months training)
     - 🟡 Intermediate (6 months - 2 years)
     - 🔴 Advanced (2+ years training)

**⏰ Time Elapsed:** ~2 minutes

---

## MINUTE 3: SET GOALS

### Step 3.1: Choose Primary Goal

Select your main fitness goal:

- 💪 **Build Muscle** - Hypertrophy focus
- 🏋️ **Gain Strength** - Power/strength focus
- 🔥 **Lose Fat** - Fat loss focus
- 🏃 **Improve Endurance** - Cardio focus
- 🎯 **General Fitness** - Balanced approach

### Step 3.2: Set Availability

How many days can you train?

- 2-3 days/week
- 4-5 days/week
- 6+ days/week

### Step 3.3: Confirm Goals

Review and click **"Save Goals"**

**⏰ Time Elapsed:** ~3 minutes

---

## MINUTE 4: EXPLORE DASHBOARD

### Your Dashboard Overview

Take 60 seconds to explore:

**Top Section:**
- 📊 Today's Activity
- 🎯 Weekly Goals
- 📈 Monthly Progress

**Middle Section:**
- 💬 Ask Coach Vitalis (AI Coach)

**Bottom Section:**
- 📝 Recent Workouts
- 🎥 Form Analysis Results
- 🏆 Recent Achievements

**Quick Actions:**
- Click **"Start Workout"** to begin training
- Click **"Ask Coach"** to chat with AI
- Click **"Video Analysis"** for form check

**⏰ Time Elapsed:** ~4 minutes

---

## MINUTE 5: START FIRST WORKOUT

### Option A: Guided Workout (Recommended for Beginners)

1. Click **"Workouts"** → **"Guided Workouts"**
2. Choose **"Beginner Full Body"**
3. Click **"Start Workout"**
4. Follow along with exercises

### Option B: Quick Workout

1. Click **"Start Workout"** (big green button)
2. Choose workout type
3. Add exercises
4. Click **"Begin"**

### Option C: Video Analysis

1. Click **"Video Analysis"**
2. Select **"Squat"**
3. Set up camera
4. Perform 3-5 reps
5. Get instant AI feedback

**⏰ Time Elapsed:** ~5 minutes
